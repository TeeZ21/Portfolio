Virgile Touzé

Silva

Project

Play the role of a forest ranger fighting to protect and preserve forests. You've been assigned to a new forest, a protected area where the ecosystem is no longer self-sufficient. Your goal is to make it self-sufficient again, by repairing the mistakes made by your peers.

Personal Feedback

This was my second VR experience, and the project where I learned the most about the platform. I realized that VR is not just a new platform, but an essential element in game design. So it was very challenging for me, as a non-VR gamer. I'm also glad to have been able to do so much research into biology to get the most complete and realistic game design possible in the time available. However, it was my first VR experience as a programmer, and I learned a lot about the Unreal Engine 5 thanks to it.

INFORMATION

Project: Prototype

Genre: Adventure / Serious Game in VR

Engine: Unreal Engine 5

Theme: VR, Serious Game, Ecology

Team Size: 3 Game Designer & 5 Game Artists

Primary Role: Game Designer & Programmer

Duration: November 2022 - December 2022 · 2 weeks

ROLES & RESPONSABILITIES

Game Designer

  • Carried out research into forest preservation

Programmer

  • Programmed all interfaces, menu, animations

  • Implemented several mini-games and interactions, including climbing and throwing
