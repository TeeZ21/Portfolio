Fury's Law

Project

Follow the story of Frank, a former LAPD investigator whose life is turned upside down after the tragic loss of his wife. Rejected by the system he once served, Frank embarks on a path of extreme violence, becoming judge and executioner.

Fury's Law is a dark, cinematic exploration of human depravity, a journey through Frank's personal hell, recalling classic themes of biblical literature in a world where violence is the only law that prevails.

Personal Feedback

Fury's Law is currently my most ambitious project. This project is a real opportunity to improve my programming skills, confronting me with challenges I've never encountered before. More complex animations and further optimization are examples of the new tasks I'm facing, surpassing the achievements of my previous projects.
