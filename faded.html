FADED

FADED is a narrative runner in which you take on the role of an aging protagonist desperately trying to piece together the fragments of his lost memory, revealing forgotten recollections at every step of this emotional adventure.

FADED was created during an internship I had the opportunity to do at HiQub Studio during my October vacation. The aim of the week was to design a pitchdeck. It was a very enriching experience, especially as it was my first professional experience in this field. It enabled me to work quickly and more intelligently on ideas, as well as presenting a product after studying its potential market.

My role: Game Designer

With: Gaetan Saura, Hugo Machado et Carla Malergue
