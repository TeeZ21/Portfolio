Luleå

Project

Immerse yourself in an epic adventure of survival and heartbreaking choices with Luleå. Play the role of a desperate father who must save his adopted son from a devastating winter nuclear apocalypse.

As bitter cold and deadly danger threaten at every turn, you will have to make crucial decisions to ensure your family's survival. Every choice you make will influence the fate of those around you, with some paths leading to heartbreaking sacrifices. Explore a frozen world full of mysteries and poignant encounters, where every step could be your last chance to save those you love. Are you ready to face nuclear winter and make tough decisions to protect your son? Your story begins now

Personal Feedback

This project was a very enriching experience for me. As I was reading Cormack McCarthy's book "The Road" at the time, I was inspired to revisit the story by integrating a system of narrative branchings, while completely renewing the characters while retaining the universe he had created.

It also gave me the opportunity to incorporate a little Swedish into the project. In fact, I'm learning the language as part of my studies, and I felt it would help bring the game's universe to life and make it more immersive.

In addition, this project gave me the opportunity to explore a new game engine, which I greatly appreciated and which fascinated me. I love discovering new technologies and tools for game development, and using Ren'Py was a very enriching experience for me.

INFORMATION

Project: Full Game

Genre: Visual Novel

Engine: Ren'Py

Team Size: Solo development

Primary Role: Narrative Designer & Programmer

Duration: December 2023 - January 2024 · 4 weeks
