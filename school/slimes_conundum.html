Slimes Conundrum

Project

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Slimes Conundrum, where you take the reins of a village populated by colorful, mischievous slimes. Your mission? Skillfully manage the population of these adorable creatures, allocating them to the right containers according to their unique characteristics. Will you rise to the challenge and become the undisputed master of slimes?

Personal Feedback

This project, completed solo and in a very short time frame, was quite intense. It taught me to focus primarily on simplicity, incorporating only one mechanic, but striving to deliver the best possible experience and the most feedback in a limited amount of time.

INFORMATION
