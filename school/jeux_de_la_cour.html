Jeux de la cour

Project

Jeux de la cour is a captivating card game that immerses players in the intriguing and cunning atmosphere of the royal courts.
Inspired by the subterfuge and political maneuvering that characterize these environments, the game focuses on bluff and cunning. Players find themselves immersed in a world where alliances are ephemeral, plots abound, and trust is a rare commodity.

Personal Feedback

Jeux de la cour was my first real card game design project. Although the project was short, it was intense, especially at a time when my experience in game design was limited. Designing a card game proved both stimulating and entertaining, especially during the regular playtesting sessions aimed at balancing the game as well as possible. It was also the first time I'd had my game tested by other people, and their enjoyment of it remains a particularly gratifying memory to this day.
