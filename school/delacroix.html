Delacroix

Project

You take on the role of assistants to the painter Eugène Delacroix, charged with providing the various elements needed to create his next masterpiece, "Liberty Leading the People". Whoever selects the most suitable components will be granted the privilege of adding his signature alongside Delacroix's.

Personal Feedback

It was my first experience of board gaming, and one of my best during my studies. It was mainly thanks to this project that I understood the importance of playtesting, especially in a board game with a monetary system to balance.
